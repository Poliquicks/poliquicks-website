Join our Teacher Workshops

We offer workshops for teachers, students, or both. See some of the topics we cover below.

  • The Importance of Voting Workshop aims to educate participants about the significance of civic engagement and the impact of their votes on local and national issues.

    By fostering a deeper understanding of the electoral process, the workshop empowers individuals to make informed decisions and actively participate in shaping their communities.

  • The "How Well Do You Know Your Local Reps" Workshop aims to educate participants on the roles and responsibilities of their local representatives, fostering greater community engagement.

  • In this workshop we tackle how you can increase civic participation through classroom activities and beyond.

  • Founding a company can be difficult to navigate. In this workshop, we cover how teachers can inspire their students to start companies and how they can best support them. In a classroom setting, we encourage them to ask questions and give them actionable advice.

  • Being a woman at a tech company can require skills in advocating for oneself, finding strong mentors, harassment, and more. We tackle best practices and how a teacher can equip their students with the right skills to navigate these challenges and create opportunity.
