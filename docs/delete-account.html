How to delete your Poliquicks account.

  1. Login to the Poliquicks app using your username and password

  2. Navigate to the Settings screen by clicking the profile icon in the top right corner of the Home screen.

  3. Tap Account

  4. Tap Delete Account

  5. Confirm your choice to delete your account by pressing Delete.

All data associated with the account will be deleted immediately. This action cannot be undone.

For additional troubleshooting, reach out to support@poliquicks.com
